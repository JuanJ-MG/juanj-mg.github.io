## Overview

This paper studies **logistical and supply-chain aspects** that influence the feasibility and architecture of a **Martian ice sample return mission**, showing how logistics considerations shape early mission design.

---

## Focus

- Mission logistics and sequencing  
- Sample handling and transportation  
- System-level coordination across mission phases  

---

*This work highlights the role of logistics-driven thinking in complex aerospace mission design.*
